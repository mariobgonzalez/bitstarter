Picture this: You will arrive to a new place (even to another country) in few weeks to complete a year abroad, to finish your studies or just because you just need a change. What is the most underrated and annoying item present in your TO-DO list? If you have been through this before you will know the answer straightaway. Those of you who are new to this, just think about the people you are going to spend your time with the most...Got it? Indeed your work colleagues or fellow students will be those people. Who are next? Flatmates. It is so easy? Just imagine the huge process of filtering, selecting, interviewing etc that comes with it.

Not so easy huh? Do not worry. We will put you in a position to do all this work in an easier way. Even in a funny way. Just fill in your criteria and preferences and add potential candidates to your circle. You will then have access to their profile, videos etc. This will speed up the proccess and will allow you to experience how this people are in reality. The endless days of interviewing or moving around different houses as an interviewee will end. Just enhace and share with people who are in the same position, make friends and have fun while finding the perfect fit. Because flatmates matters. Flatmatters.

FAQ

What is Flatmatters?
A social network focused on connecting people who are looking for flatmates. Maybe more.
Why Flatmatters?
Because we would like to speed up the process of finding new flatmates. In the end of the classical, long and boring process you might end up with a bad choice. We do not guarantee a good choice. But it will be easier and funnier.
And also... BECAUSE it MATTERS!
